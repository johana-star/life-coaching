Typography

Two typefaces — serif for display, sans-serif for body

Display — Cormorant Garamond 300

h1 · clamp(2.6rem, 5vw, 4rem) · tracking −0.01em · leading 1.1

A headline that speaks directly to the reader.

Section heading — Cormorant Garamond 300

h2 · clamp(2rem, 3.5vw, 2.8rem) · leading 1.2

A section heading that sets the tone — specific and honest.

Sub-heading — Cormorant Garamond 300

h3 · clamp(1.8rem, 3vw, 2.4rem) · leading 1.2

A sub-heading that introduces a topic or card group.

Card heading — DM Sans 500

h4 · 1rem · weight 500

Card or step heading — concise and actionable

Body — DM Sans 300

p · 0.95–1.05rem · leading 1.75–1.85

Body copy is set in DM Sans at 300 weight. It is warm and direct — written to be read by someone who may be tired, overwhelmed, or skeptical. Line height is generous. Sentences are honest.

Label / eyebrow — DM Sans 500

0.72rem · weight 500 · tracking 0.14em · uppercase

Section label · Location

Trust bar — DM Sans 500

0.75rem · weight 500 · tracking 0.08em · uppercase

Trust signal · Identity · Community

Disclaimer / note — DM Sans 300

0.8rem · leading 1.6 · sakura left border · matches btn-ghost border style

A clarifying note or disclaimer — smaller, quieter text with a sakura left border to mark it as distinct from body copy. Note visually corresponds with btn-ghost.

Nav CTA — DM Sans 400

0.8rem · weight 400 · tracking 0.06em · uppercase

Primary action

tag-highlight (sakura tones) blends into the sakura section background — the #about override substitutes horizon background with coastal border and abyss text. All four tag variants pass AA contrast on pine.

Inline SVG badges sized to cap height of surrounding text. Use alt text for accessibility. Colors are hardcoded to specific palette tokens which roughly correspond to pride flag colors.
